About

My name is Kyle. I design things—websites, mostly—and enjoy finding the most elegant ways to harmonize content with form. That’s a fancy way of saying I take text and colors and images and I arrange them in a way that immediately makes sense.

I have done this professionally for seven years; first freelance, then in an agency, and most recently as a product designer at Dropbox. I believe that the best products are well-designed inside and out, from the naming of variables and the file architecture to the copywriting and visuals. Everything matters.

I spend my spare time hiking, snapping photos, and practicing lots of Debussy and Chopin. I’m passionate about anything related to social reform, environmental change, and the arts. I’m not taking on new projects at the moment, but I’m always happy to grab drinks and talk shop with anyone who’s interested. Email me and let’s get talking. ✌️
